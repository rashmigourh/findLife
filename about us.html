About us

Welcome to Find Life, Our motto is "Unlock the potential of a healthier You with Us. One step at a time, for a future of wellness and vitality." Our website is a trusted source for reliable health information and wellness guidance. Our mission is to empower individuals with the knowledge and tools they need to make informed decisions about their health and well-being. Along with this our website also predict about various illness and diseases the person is suffering from by using our prediction model. For this, we gather relevant medical data for predicting various diseases. This could include factors like age, gender, blood pressure, cholesterol levels, family history, smoking habits, diabetes status, etc, then pass it to the deployed machine learning model via the API. Receive the prediction response and display it to the user.

Our Vision

At Find Life, we envision a world where everyone has access to accurate and up-to-date health information. We believe that informed individuals are better equipped to take control of their health, prevent illnesses, and lead fulfilling lives and we make this possible by predicting your health and providing you accurate and up-to-date information about your disease, its symptoms and causes, and its prevention. We are dedicated to bringing you well-researched and evidence-based content and are committed to providing information that is accurate, trustworthy, and easily understandable, ensuring that you have the resources you need to make healthy choices.

About our project

Me Rashmi Gourh and my friend Riya Dubey together worked on this project which tells predictions based on some input parameters like gender, age, various diseases, and smoking status. Here we have mainly worked on heart stroke prediction since according to the World Health Organization (WHO) stroke is the 2nd leading cause of death globally, responsible for approximately 11% of total deaths. This project is based on the concept to predict whether a patient is likely to get a stroke or not. This website will give you a detailed explanation regarding heart stroke and other various illnesses, what all symptoms you will get, how it is caused, and the prevention steps that you can take to stay healthy or if it is too serious then what all treatments are recommended for it. As we all know to live a healthy life you should know your risk and what healthy lifestyle you can follow to stay fit. Our website and project can help you to build a powerful prevention plan with your healthcare team (doctors, nurses, pharmacists, registered dietitians, and other professionals).



Here we havehtml, css, bootstrap, javascipt for our front-end work, phython,flask and machine learning for analysing and predicting our dataset and back-end work.


  • Rashmi Gourh: Done the front end work and created this website FindLife using html, css, javascript, bootstrap.

  • Riya Dubey: Done the back end work, used python and machine learning knowledge to analyse and predict the dataset and its accuracy to get the result.
